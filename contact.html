Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you service?

We provide our services throughout South Africa, with our main service areas being Gauteng, Mpumalanga, North West, and Free State provinces. For projects in other regions, please contact us to discuss logistics and availability.

How long does it take to complete a custom order?

Production time varies depending on the complexity and size of your order. Small to medium projects typically take 1-2 weeks, while larger, more complex orders may require 3-4 weeks. We always provide estimated completion dates when you place your order.

Do you provide installation services?

Yes, we offer professional installation services for most of our products, ensuring proper fitting and functionality. Our skilled team can travel to your location to complete the installation. Installation fees vary based on the project scope and location.

What warranty do your products come with?

Our products come with a standard warranty that varies by product type. Most items carry a 1-2 year warranty against manufacturing defects and premature material failure under normal use conditions. Extended warranties are available for certain products at an additional cost.

Can I request custom colors or branding on my order?

Absolutely! We offer custom colors to match your branding requirements. Additionally, we can add logos, text, and other branding elements to most of our products. Our design team will work with you to ensure your branding is accurately represented.
